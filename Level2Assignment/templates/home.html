ABOUT US

When life was set, when the lorries they owned were running for different booking offices, the four men from Mallasamudram dared to have a dream. A dream of opening their own booking office and manage their own fleet of lorries. With a lot of hardwork and perseverance, they opened up an office and travelled all over Tamilnadu to get clients. With honesty and unparalleled dedication, they established a successful business.

Here, we are 30 years later, with four offices across Tamilnadu, standing strong in the business. With the next generation taking over the services, there is progress with latest technologies and trust with traditional values.

OUR MISSION & VISSION

Our mission is provide the best transportation services which is safe, reliable and efficient with commitment to customer's needs and operational excellence.

Our vision is to be an efficient part of supplychain and create value to the economy with superior services.
